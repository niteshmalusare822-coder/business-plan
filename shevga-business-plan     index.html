Part A: Shevga Patti Powder Process
1
Patti Collection (Harvest)
Subah 6–9 baje patti todo — fresh, green, disease-free. Month mein 2–3 baar harvest possible.
🌿 1 acre shevga tree se ~500–800 kg fresh patti/year milti hai
2
Cleaning & Washing
Fresh patti ko saaf paani mein dholo. Stems (danthi) alag karo — sirf leaves use karo powder ke liye.
⚠️ Stems mein fiber zyada, powder quality ghatata hai
3
Shade Drying (1st Stage)
Direct sun mein mat sookao! Pehle 4–6 ghante chhaanv mein sookao — Vitamin C protect hota hai.
🌡️ Temperature: 40–50°C ideal
4
Solar Dryer (2nd Stage)
Raheja solar dryer mein spread karo. 2–3 din mein patti completely dry ho jaati hai. Moisture 5–8% tak aata hai.
✅ 10 kg fresh patti → 1 kg dry patti
5
Grinding / Pulverizing
Dry patti ko pulverizer mein daalo. Fine powder milta hai — sieve se 80–100 mesh filter karo.
Color: Dark green = good quality
6
Quality Check
Moisture, color, aur smell check karo. Green color, no smell = premium grade. Dark brown = low grade.
🧪 NABL lab test (export ke liye): ₹5,000–8,000
7
Packing & Sealing
100g / 250g / 500g food-grade pouches mein pack karo. Sealing machine se seal karo. Label lagao.
📦 Shelf life: 12–18 months (proper packing mein)
8
Storage
Cool, dry, dark jagah store karo. Direct sunlight se door rakho. Temperature: below 25°C.
🏪 Ready for local sell / export
Part B: Shevga Sange (Pods) Process
1
Fresh Pod Harvest
25–30 cm ki green, tender pods todo. Too mature pods mein fiber zyada hota hai.
🫛 Fresh pods directly sell — local sabzi market
2
Grading
Size aur quality ke hisab se sort karo: A grade (30cm+), B grade (20–30cm), C grade (reject).
A grade = premium price
3
Solar Drying (Dried Pods ke liye)
Pods ko slice karke solar dryer mein 3–4 din sookao. Dried pods shelf life 6+ months.
Fresh: ₹20-40/kg → Dried: ₹80-120/kg — 3x value!
4
Packing
Dried pods ko airtight bags mein pack karo. Export ke liye vacuum pack karo.
500g / 1kg SKUs best chalta hai
📊 Conversion Ratios (Important!)
• 10 kg fresh patti → 1 kg powder
• 5 kg fresh pods → 1 kg dried pods
• 1 kg powder (retail) = ₹200–500
• Raw material cost: ₹5–15/kg fresh leaves
